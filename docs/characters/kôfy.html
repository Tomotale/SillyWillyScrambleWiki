Large poke that covers a section of the screen. Causes enough tumble to combo into a 41236K followup. Kôfy stays in place during S version. In H version, Kôfy moves with the opponent, but has much more whiff recovery. If S or H is pressed again, Kôfy will perform a vertical poke to catch opponents jumping over the initial attack. Use carefully as this follow-up is highly punishable, even if the initial attack was blocked. Launches the opponent high into the air on counter hit, launches only slightly on other hits. Kôfy stays in place during S follow-up. In the H follow-up, Kôfy jumps to meet the opponent.
