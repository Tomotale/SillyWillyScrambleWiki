Osi (Back to Home)

Osi is a shapeshifting fighter who uses flames to get in close and smoke to disorient the opponent. With powerful mixup tools, and the ability to impair the opponents vision, clever setup of smoke is essential to playing this character. With access to Burning Resolve, Osi gains access to high damage combos and fast approaches as well. Make sure to use this all together to thoroughly beat down your opponent.

Unique Mechanics

Mist

Osi's signature mechanic is Mist. Certain moves like Smoke Wheel deploy clouds of Mist, which Osi can use to obscure himself from the opponent, only showing a faint outline of his form. This makes it much harder for opponents to see and react to Osi's movements. When Osi is inside the Mist, he takes 50% more damage from attacks, and if he is hit three times while in the Mist, it will dissipate. Mist, unless created by Shrouded in Mist, will also fade after a certain amount of time. While the Mist obscures Osi, it does not do the same for his visual effects, meaning he should stick to using moves with less pronounced effects if he wishes to conceal himself effectively.

Ignited Blade
236P
Damage Guard Startup Active Recovery OnBlock Invuln
- All - - - -
The beginning of Osi's rekka. Osi transforms his hand into a flaming blade and slashes forward. This move can be cancelled into one of three different follow-up attacks by pressing P, K, or S after the initial hit connects, allowing him access to a high, low, or an evasive option respectively. Like all moves in his rekka, this move creates a large amount of visual noise, making it considerably dangerous and less preferable to use inside of Mist as a mix-up tool.

The low follow-up to Ignited Blade. Osi reverts his hand back to normal and ignites his leg, sweeping it along the ground. Generally safer and faster than its overhead counterpart, but for less in return. Effective at catching opponents who are mashing expecting to hit the startup of 236P~P or trying to hard-punish 236P~S.

Osi takes a moment to gather himself, before engulfing himself in flame. In this state, Osi's movement speed is greatly increased, and every attack now sets the opponent on fire, dealing bonus damage over time. This state lasts for a limited time.

Shrouded in Mist
632146P with 50% meter
The fog is coming...
Damage Guard Startup Active Recovery OnBlock Invuln
- - -
Osi lets out a powerful burst of smoke that fills the screen. Unlike other moves that deploy Mist, the Mist created from this move lasts indefinitely until Osi is hit three times. Simultaneously a blessing and a curse, as while Osi can now move around the screen obscured, the opponent gains more opportunities to hit him while he is in the Mist, taking advantage of the increased damage he takes while inside it. Likewise, Osi can use this fact as an advantage, baiting the opponent into using riskier, more committal moves that can be easily punished.
  • Covers the entire screen in Mist on activation
  • Mist lasts indefinitely and will not expire over time
