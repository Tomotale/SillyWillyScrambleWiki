Guiblo (Back to Home)

Despite his status as a grappler, Guiblo boasts a plethora of tools that help him dominate the match. When utilized correctly, Guiblo can very easily capitalize on the opponent's whiff for great reward.

Guiblo's strategy mainly revolves around controlling the opponent's position using tools such as Gone Fishin' or his long range normals. Once Guiblo has secured a position, he can can close in to strike the fear of god into the opponent's hearts coupled with his oppressive pressure. Boasting incredible air control, terrifying ground control, and a powerful parry, it will be very easy for Guiblo to condition the opponent to act according to his favor.

Guiblo's main weakness is that despite his immovable offense, he doesn't hold much in the way of good defensive options. He only has command grab supers, both of which could be easily outclassed by a relatively well-placed strike. Additionally, while his meterless Huebenly Buster has invulnerability on startup, it won't serve him well in the face of a regular pressure sequence. Guiblo must instead rely on hard-calling using his backdash or his parry to steal back his turn.
