Unique Mechanics

Cursor State

A state that the opponent can be placed in when hit by 41236S. While in this state, all items that perform attacks such as Funny Fork, Blue Bird, and Fishing Rod all home into the opponent's current position. Additionally, when drawing an item with 236P, Goopert can influence the direction of the falling item using 6 or 4 to pull the item forwards or backwards respectively. This state lasts for a set amount of time or until Goopert is hit.

Items

Goopert's items come out in random order, but the next three will always be displayed on the HUD.

AK-47 (Hold OK)
5P
Damage Guard Startup Active Recovery OnBlock Invuln
-xN All - - - -
When Goopert grabs this item, his 5P is replaced with a hitscan bullet shot aimed directly at the opponent. Goopert is given a limited amount of ammo, and must fire a minimum of three bullets per use. Goopert can continue fire by holding down P either until the button is released or the gun is out of ammo. Goopert is free to move around while shooting. If picked up by the opponent, they will instead gain 50% meter.
  • The AK-47's magazine contains 30 rounds of ammunition.

Smoke Bomb
When this item hits the ground, a smoke cloud will appear and cover a set area. Neither the opponent nor Goopert is visible in this smoke, allowing unseen mix-ups from Goopert or the opponent.

Coffee Cup
When Goopert or the opponent grabs this item, their speed is raised by 15%, and they get a passive meter gain over time. If this item is picked up by Max specifically, they will gain 1 Caffeine.

Funny Fork
Damage Guard Startup Active Recovery OnBlock Invuln
- All (Guard Crush) - - - -
When this item is drawn, it immediately flies horizontally across the screen to hit the opponent. Inflicts Guard Crush on block.

Blue Bird
Damage Guard Startup Active Recovery OnBlock Invuln
- High - - - -
When this item is drawn, it immediately flies diagonally upwards, comes to a stop, then suddenly dives downwards. Hits as an overhead.

Broken Clock
Damage Guard Startup Active Recovery OnBlock Invuln
0 All 10 120 - -
When this item is drawn, Goopert can only block. After 10 frames, time freezes, hitting anyone who wasn't blocking at that moment. Time resumes after a couple seconds.
  • This item cannot be discarded with 236[P].

Fishing Rod
Hook... ... line... ... and sinker!
Version Damage Guard Startup Active Recovery OnBlock Invuln
Cast - All - - - -
Reel - All - - - -
Explosion - All - - - -
When this item is drawn, Goopert equips an angler's hat and casts a fishing line with his rod. The fishing hook travels the length of the full screen. On contact with an opponent or at the max length of the line, Goopert reels it in. At any point in this attack, Goopert can explode the hook using P, even if the attack whiffs.
  • The hook can be deflected with any strike or projectile attack, delaying the final reel-in.

Goopert²
When this item is drawn, a secondary Goopert appears from the top of the screen, landing in a random position. Once landed, this Goopert randomly performs one of Goopert's specials (including an additional behold!). Disappears after a few seconds or if behold! was used, when the drawn item's duration is over.

O-Tome
Version Damage Guard Startup Active Recovery OnBlock Invuln
Reflector - All - - - -

When Goopert or the opponent grabs this item, they immediately perform Tomo's 6S. At the end of the initial reflection, the wall transforms into a wall of ice. This wall only disappears when hit by the opponent, and Goopert is able to pass through it.
