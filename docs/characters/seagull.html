Seagull (Back to Home)

Seagull is a rushdown character with tight air movement who focuses on air combos and tricky movement to overwhelm the opponent. With an astonishing 3 air jumps and incredible mobility, Seagull is able to stay out of reach and take advantage when the opportunity presents itself.

Seagull

Type

Command Normals

Special Attacks

Scam (Air OK)
214S

Seagull lets out a large scream with a cartoon speech bubble appearing behind him and hitting the opponent from afar. If used in the air, Seagull retains his previous air momentum.

Bet
j.236P/S/H

Seagull quickly dashes through the air, hitting anything in his path. P version sends Seagull diagonally upwards, S version sends Seagull horizontally, and H version sends Seagull diagonally downwards. All versions of Bet hit overhead. Using S or H Bet close to the ground combined with the threat of a possible Skill allows Seagull to perform an incredbly ambiguous overhead.

Skill
236K

Seagull shoots himself downwards at a steep angle with no hitbox. He can use this move to quickly cross through the opponent for a scary three-way mixup, or to escape a dire situation with low commitment.
