Unique Mechanics

Exhilaration

Dani starts the round with 0% Exhilaration Meter. The Exhilaration Meter can be filled by dealing damage to the opponent. Exhilaration is a state that greatly enhances Dani's movement speed, damage output, and changes her moveset—making her no longer reliant on her limited resource, Ammo. Exhilaration is entered automatically when the Exhilaration Meter maxes out, or manually by using Ace. While in Exhilaration, the Exhilaration Meter decreases over time. If the meter reaches 0% without Dani exiting Exhilaration manually using Ace, she will instead be forced out of Exhilaration and into a brief vulnerable period of recovery.

Ammo

Dani's normal state gives her great tools to open up the opponent, but not without its limitations. Dani is limited to only 8 bullets of Ammo, which are consumed by using certain special moves. Once out of ammo, Dani is unable to use these special moves until she enters Exhilaration, which fully restocks her ammo on exit.
