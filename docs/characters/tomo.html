Tomo (Back to Home)

Weilding a glaive and tome, Tomo focuses on keeping the opponent at a safe distance, allowing him to then close the gap and convert into full combos up close.

Unique Mechanics

Mana

Mana is a resource that Tomo spends to perform his specials. Upon being hit, he loses Mana equal to the HP damage that he takes. He cannot use specials that cost more Mana then he has. Upon hitting 0 Mana, enter Stagger state, then regain all Mana.

Stagger State

When Tomo's mana is exhausted, he automatically enters Stagger State. Command Normals

6S
6S with 5% Mana

Tomo uses his tome to quickly summon a wall of magic that not only reflects any incoming projectiles. In addition to its strong defensive capabilities, this move can be cancelled into itself on hit, rewarding Tomo with easy damage in corner combos.

Special Attacks

Ignis Rush (Hold OK)
236S/H with 20% Mana

A chargable fire-based projectile that travels along a set path, exploding into a firery vortex which launches the opponent up into the air.

  • S Ignis Rush creates a projectile that travels along the ground. The explosion triggers after a certain amount of distance or upon hitting the opponent.
  • H Ignis Rush creates a projectile that travels diagonally into the air. The explosion no longer triggers automatically, only activating when it hits the opponent.

Ventus Thrust
214S/H with 10% Mana

Tomo summons blades of wind that can be used to push the opponent.

  • S Ventus Thrust summons blades of wind from the opposite side of the screen, pushing the opponent towards Tomo. Can catch opponents who have a habit of backdashing. Pressing S again makes Tomo perform a forward thrust with his spear, sending the opponent into the winds.
  • H Ventus Thrust summons blades directly in front of Tomo, sending the opponent away. Pressing H again causes the blades to switch direction and move diagonally.

Renovare
22P/K

Tomo stands in place and recovers his Mana gauge

  • P Renovare recovers mana with no cost, but at a slow speed. Is very easily counterhit, so Tomo should be very careful when using it.
  • K Renovare recovers mana quickly at the cost of meter.

Glacies Pierce
214P/K with 15% Mana

Tomo covers a large amount of space with ice, interrupting the opponent's approach. On hit, freezes the opponent in place until a set amount of time passes or immediately upon the next hit.

  • In P Glacies Pierce, Tomo slams the ground in front of him, sprouting a large pillar of ice that hits the opponent low. Creates a large enough barrier to make it difficult for the opponent to jump over.
  • In K Glacies Pierce, Tomo rides into the air, summoning a trail of ice underneath him that hits the opponent high. On air hit, the opponent is frozen until they reach the ground, taking additional damage and causing a ground bounce. The ice trail is summoned high enough to where it is difficult to jump over, and low enough that the opponent needs to use a good low-profile option to respond.
Boasting its large space control and relatively low mana cost, Glacies Pierce is a formidable move that demands respect in neutral, making it simultaneously one of Tomo's safest options to set up Renovare and a great option for setting up damage using a fully charged Ignis Rush.
